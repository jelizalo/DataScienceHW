Is there a correlation between excellent health and possessing a Bachelor Degree?

Correlation Between Reported Excellent Health and Possession of Bachelor Degree

This graph illustrates the correlaton between reported excellent health and has graduated with a Bachelor Degree. The used data is based on the year 2014 and was collected through the American Community Survey (ACS) and the Behavioral Risk Factor Surveillance System (BRFSS). Each marker represents an individual state in the United States.

The calculated correlation between reported excellent health and possessing a Bachelor Degree is 0.519897842.
